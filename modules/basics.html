Module 1: Web Basics

Understanding the Internet

The Internet is like a big network that connects computers all around the world! Think of it as a huge library where everyone can share information.

Fun Activity: Internet Explorer Game

Draw a map of how you think information travels from your computer to your friend's computer in another village.

Your First HTML Tags

HTML tags are like special instructions that tell the computer how to show your webpage. Let's learn some basic tags:

<h1>This is a big heading</h1>
<p>This is a paragraph of text</p>
<img src="picture.jpg" alt="A beautiful picture">

Practice Time!

Try creating a webpage about your community using these tags:

  1. Add a heading with your village/city name
  2. Write a paragraph about what makes your community special
  3. Add a list of your favorite places

Community Challenge

Create a simple webpage that shows important information about your community:

  • Local market days
  • Important phone numbers
  • Community events
