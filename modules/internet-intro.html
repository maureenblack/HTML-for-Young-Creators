The Magic of the Internet! 🌟

What is the Internet? 🌐

Imagine a giant network of computers all connected together, like a huge spider web that goes around the whole world! This is the Internet!

🏠 🏫 🏢 ━━━

Just like roads connect houses and schools, the Internet connects computers!

How Does It Work? 🤔

1

Your Message Starts Here! 📱

When you want to see a website, your computer sends a message asking for it.

2

Through the Internet Highway! 🛣️

Your message travels through special cables (like super-fast highways) to reach the website's computer.

3

Website Sends Back! 🎁

The website's computer sends back all the pictures and text you want to see.

4

Magic Appears! ✨

Your computer receives everything and shows you the website!
